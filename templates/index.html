About

What is Cancer?

Cancer is a group of diseases involving abnormal cell growth with the potential to invade or spread to other parts of the body.

Early Detection

Early detection of cancer increases the chances of successful treatment and can save lives.

Symptoms

Common symptoms of cancer include unexplained weight loss, fatigue, and changes in skin appearance.

Treatment Options

Treatment options vary based on the type and stage of cancer, including surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation therapy.
